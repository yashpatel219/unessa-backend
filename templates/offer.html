Annexure A: Terms and Conditions of Internship

The following terms and conditions shall govern your internship engagement with Unessa Foundation:

  1. Position & Reporting Structure
    You are being appointed as a Crowdfunding Intern at Unessa Foundation. A designated mentor will connect with you to provide guidance and evaluate your progress throughout the internship.

Your primary responsibility is to raise funds for the organization through your personal and extended network. Additionally, you may contribute to outreach, research, campaign execution, or other support tasks based on project needs.

  1. Internship Commencement and Duration
    Your internship will commence on and shall continue for a period of one (1) month unless otherwise extended or curtailed based on mutual agreement and performance.
  2. Mode of Work
    This internship will be conducted remotely, unless specified otherwise. You are expected to maintain regular communication with your mentor via digital channels such as WhatsApp, Zoom, or email.
  3. Time Commitment
    You are expected to devote sincere and consistent effort during your tenure. Any planned unavailability must be communicated and approved in advance to ensure alignment with project deliverables.
  4. Confidentiality & Data Security
    You shall maintain strict confidentiality regarding any information—verbal, written, or digital—that you come across during your internship. This includes data related to beneficiaries, partners, donors, internal reports, strategies, or any intellectual property of Unessa Foundation. Any breach of confidentiality or data misuse will be treated as a serious violation, resulting in immediate termination of the internship and possible legal recourse.
  5. Ownership of Work
    All content, research, materials, and intellectual property generated by you during the internship shall remain the exclusive property of Unessa Foundation. You shall not reuse, reproduce, or share this work outside the organization without written permission.
  6. Communication Expectations
    You are required to respond promptly to communications from your mentor and the organization. Regular updates on your progress must be shared when requested. Two-way, timely communication is critical to the success of this internship.
  7. Professional Conduct
    You are expected to conduct yourself with integrity, respect, and professionalism in all interactions—whether with team members, beneficiaries, or external stakeholders. Behaviour deemed inappropriate, unethical, or unprofessional may lead to immediate termination of the internship.
  8. Termination Clause
    Either party may terminate the internship by giving a seven (7) day written notice, without the need to cite specific reasons. However, the organization reserves the right to terminate your internship immediately in the event of misconduct, violation of terms, or breach of confidentiality.
  9. Certification of Completion
    Upon successful completion of the internship, fulfilling all assigned responsibilities to the satisfaction of your mentor and the organization, you shall be awarded an Internship Completion Certificate.
    ➤ Please note: This certificate must accompany this offer letter for the internship to be deemed valid and effective for any formal submission or recognition in professional or academic institutions.
  10. Commitment to Impact
    Unessa Foundation nurtures individuals who strive to go beyond what’s expected. We highly value creative thinking, leadership, self-motivation, and a genuine commitment to serve society. This internship is your canvas—surprise us with your ideas, passion, and drive.
  11. Performance & Incentives
    This is a paid internship. You will receive a stipend equal to 20% of the total verified funds you raise, payable at the end of your internship.

Incentives are performance-based, and the complete structure is available on your intern dashboard. The more impact you create, the more you earn.

 

We hope this internship becomes a defining chapter in your journey of purpose and impact.
